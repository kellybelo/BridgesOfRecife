The bridges of Recife mean much more than a link between islands and beautiful post cards. These constructions marked the history of the city and have a strong identification with everyday life. The bridges were built with different materials and architecture due to the influence of the settlers who came here.

Recife is the fifth-largest metropolitan area in Brazil with 3,743,854 inhabitants, the largest metropolitan area of the North / Northeast Regions, and the capital of the state of Pernambuco.
